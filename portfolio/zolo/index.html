Zolo Household Budget Template:

It can be hard to know how to budget for house expenses. What are typical household budget items? How much should you spend each month? To help we created this free interactive house expense calculator.

To get the most accurate benchmark costs and the typical earnings for your country, select your country (either Canada or America) from the drop down menu at the top on the right.

To use the Zolo Household Budget Calculator, follow these instructions:

  1. Select your country of residence
  2. Click on the arrow in the section you want to complete
  3. Choose the income or expense you want and click ‘Include’ button
  4. A benchmark price will show up along with a price bar. Add your income or expense. If you don’t know, add the benchmark income or expense that shows up above the price bar. Not all earning sources are costs have benchmarks
  5. If you make a mistake, just re-enter the correct amount. If you wish to erase the income source or expense, click the ‘X’ button
  6. Repeat this process until all income sources and costs are included. A pie-chart of where your money is allocated will begin to show on the right, along with a summary of your housing costs, in relation to your earnings.
